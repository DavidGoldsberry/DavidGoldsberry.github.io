TL;DR?

Indie Comic Ninja is a project dedicated to getting independant, underground, & small press comics into your hands via a subscription service.

About

We know you can't make it to all the cons ever. Even if you could there's so much to do that meeting all the great small press creators is maybe not as high on your itinerary. Then comes Indie Comic Ninja. We will be a subscription service that will collect great low budget comics from creators all over america and mail them to your doorstep.

When will this goodness begin?

We're still in the beginning phases, sign up for our newsletter to find out with the first batches will be sent out and to get your early bird discounts and bonuses.
